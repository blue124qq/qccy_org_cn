Company Claims

Important Notice Prior To Submitting Your Claim

  • As of 24/03/2020, claims are no longer being accepted by post.
  • Submit claim documentation in Word®, Excel® or PDF format only by email to  IndustryGrantClaims@enterprise-ireland.com

Remember, when emailing, to include your Company Name and Project Number in the Subject Line. 

Use your Client Number (CES ID) number when encrypting files. Your CES ID number can be found in “Schedule A” of your company’s Letter of Offer.

Any Industry grant claim queries should be emailed to GrantPaymentsQueries@enterprise-ireland.com

To assist us, please state your six digit grant project number (e.g. 123456/RR) and your company name as shown in your Letter of Offer

Please note that a grant claim should not be submitted to us if any of the required information detailed on the “Grant Claim Checklist” is missing or marked as "to follow". Incomplete claims will be deemed invalid and returned unprocessed.

For new clients or where the Grantee company’s existing bank details have changed

Please submit the new Electronic Fund Transfer (EFT)/BANK details to

  • our Finance Department at bank.confirmation@enterprise-ireland.com attaching supporting verification of these details on bank headed paper.

A member of our Finance Department team will contact you by phone to verify the bank details - note that, you will only be requested to confirm the last 4 digits of your IBAN/account no.

Enterprise Ireland's Commitment to GDPR

Enterprise Ireland is committed to its transparency obligations under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our data protection notice for personal data that is supplied to us by our clients is available here. This notice contains important information about how we process personal data that is supplied to us by clients. We request that you read the notice carefully and that you ensure that it is made available to any data subjects (e.g. your employees) whose personal data you provide to us.
