Ireland's Space Industry Directory

Ireland has a long-established history of astronomy and space research. In recent years, Ireland has developed a thriving space industry and research community, facilitated primarily through Ireland’s membership of the European Space Agency (ESA).

For information on the growing space-related capabilities in Irish industry, please consult Ireland's Space Industry Directory below.

Enterprise Ireland also provides a source of expertise for Irish companies in developing and executing their respective space strategies as well as being a point of reference for international space industry wishing to identify relevant sources of space-related expertise within Ireland.

For further information on Ireland’s National Space Strategy for Enterprise, details of the ESA programmes that Ireland participates in, and contact details for the ESA team members, go to Enterprise Ireland’s ESA page here .
